Player temperature changes based on:
* biome temperature
* rain/snow
* wetness
* clothing
* altitude
* nearby heat source
* [[Seasons]]
* hunger/fatigue

**Simple Formula Concept**
Body Temp Change = Biome Temp + Clothing Protection + Heat Source Bonus - Wetness Penalty - Altitude Penalty - Storm Penalty

**Status Effects**
Cold Stage 1
* minor slowness
* faster hunger drain

Cold Stage 2
* mining fatigue
* shaking/blur
* stamina loss

Cold Stage 3
* damage over time

Heat Stage 1
* faster thirst drain

Heat Stage 2
* slower stamina recovery

Heat Stage 3
* damage over time
