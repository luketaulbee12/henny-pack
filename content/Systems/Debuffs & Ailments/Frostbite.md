State of [[Body Temperature System]]

Prolonged exposure to cold can cause player to develop frostbite which causes:
* Slowness
* Negative draw to [[Stamina & Fatigue]]
* Increased hunger
* Increased damage taken from mobs

Can be avoided by:
* wearing proper clothing (soaked clothes will dramatically increase ability to get frostbite),
* managing internal temperature and wetness correctly, 
* eating hot meals.
