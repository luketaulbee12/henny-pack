Clothing, Armor, and overall inventory items contribute to weight

Three levels of weight and their effects:

Light:
* no effect

Medium:
* faster stamina drain

Heavy:
* movement slowdown

Overloaded:
* cannot sprint
* injury risk

Transport Recipes
[[Backpack]]
Leather + String + Chest = Backpack

[[Sled]]
Planks + Sticks + Rope = Sled

[[Cart]]
Boat + Wheels + Chest = Hand Cart

[[Wheel]]
Planks + Stick = Wheel

Players should prioritize building logistics networks like rail or water
