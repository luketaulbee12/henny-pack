Certain trees can be tapped for resin
- Spruce
- Dark Oak 
- Jungle 

Process for obtaining resin:
* Stripped tree log (must be an actual tree)
- Place [[Tap]]
- Connect minecraft bucket
- Raw Resin accumulates into bucket over 7 minecraft days
- Raw resin bucket must be placed on a campfire where it boils down into [[Resin]] and bucket is returned to player.
