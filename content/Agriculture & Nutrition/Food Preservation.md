Foods spoil at different rates depending on their type and how they have been stored.

Proper care must be taken to ensure food stays good.

[[Drying Rack]] can be used to smoke any meat variety and increase its freshness 2x

[[Salt]] can be applied to any meat variety to increase its freshness by 3x.

In combination these increase food good by date by 5x.

[[Grain Silo]] can be made to store farmed foods and preserve them for longer
