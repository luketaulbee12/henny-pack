
A balanced diet is required to stay healthy.

Three main categories:
- protein
	- Meats
	- Fish
- fat
	- Animal Fat
	- Butter
	- Milk
	- Butter
- carbohydrates
	- Bread
	- Potatoes
	- Grain
	- Berries

Poor nutrition causes:
- slower healing
- faster fatigue
- eventual poison effect
- worse cold resistance
